Our Achievements

Team Sheldon has earned awards in both FTC and FRC, including Inspire, Think, and Engineering Design. Our achievements reflect our passion for robotics, innovation, and teamwork on every level.

2025 - MRT - FRC

After missing Southern Cross due to Cyclone Alfred, we brought our modified 2025 Kitbot to the field — and it delivered. We placed 9th in the Qualification Rankings and were honoured to lead as Alliance Captain for the 7th Alliance.

A massive milestone for our team and an unforgettable off-season event!

2024 - Design Award - FTC

Team Sheldon has proudly received the Design Award in the 2024 FIRST Tech Challenge! This honour celebrates our team’s creativity, innovation, and dedication to a professional, well-executed design process — a true reflection of the hard work and passion behind our robot.
