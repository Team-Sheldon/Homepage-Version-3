About Us

Team Sheldon is Sheldon College’s premier robotics team, proudly competing in FIRST Robotics (FRC & FTC). We’re a crew of passionate students who blend engineering, coding, design, and strategy to build competitive robots — and a better future. But we’re more than just machines and matches. Through outreach, innovation, and teamwork, we aim to spark a love for STEM in our community and empower the next generation of creators and problem-solvers.

About First (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology)

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global nonprofit organization that inspires young people to be science and technology leaders. Through exciting mentor-based programs, FIRST builds self-confidence, knowledge, and life skills in young people. The organization’s mission is to engage students in hands-on learning experiences that foster innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.
