FIRST® Robotics Competition - 2025

Team Sheldon proudly competed as Team 9996 – Off-Season Demo Team 9996 at the 2025 Melbourne Robotics Tournament, held at Blackburn High School, VIC. This off-season event, part of the FIRST Robotics Competition calendar, brought together passionate robotics teams from across the country for a weekend of strategic gameplay, engineering excellence, and team spirit.

Originally, we had planned to compete at the 2025 Southern Cross Regional; however, due to the impact of Cyclone Alfred, we were unfortunately unable to attend. Determined not to miss out on the chance to put our hard work to the test, we set our sights on MRT 2025 as an alternative—and we’re proud to say it was well worth it.

Despite competing with a modified 2025 Kitbot, our robot performed exceptionally. We finished 9th in the Qualification Rankings and were honoured to serve as the Alliance Captain for the 7th Alliance in the elimination rounds.

This experience was an incredible opportunity to develop our technical skills, strengthen our teamwork, and proudly represent Sheldon College. You can check out our official event results here:
