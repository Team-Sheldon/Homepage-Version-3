FIRST® Tech Challenge - 2024

Team Sheldon proudly competed as Sheldon1 at the 2024 Brisbane South Qualifier, held on Saturday, 16 November at St Peters Lutheran College in Indooroopilly, QLD. The event, known by its code AUINQ , was part of Week 10 since kickoff and brought together some of the brightest young robotics minds in the region. With months of design, testing, and problem-solving behind us, our robot hit the field ready to perform—and it did not disappoint. We were thrilled to be awarded the Design Award , a huge recognition of our team’s creativity, engineering process, and commitment to professional design principles. It was a proud moment streamed live for the world to see, and one we won’t soon forget.
